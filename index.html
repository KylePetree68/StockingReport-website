About This Site

New Mexico Game and Fish stocking reports provide valuable information to anglers, but digging through past reports to find when your favorite spot was last stocked can be a challenge. Additionally, the official site only keeps the last four reports online, making it difficult to track history.

StockingReport.com solves this problem by collecting the official data and making it searchable for any body of water in the state. We build a permanent, growing history so you can easily find the date, species, size, and quantity for every stocking event.

We hope you find it useful!
