🎣 Recent Activity

In the last 6 months, Cimarron River (West Of Cimarron) has been stocked 1 time with 2,420 Triploid Rainbow Trout, averaging 10.4 inches. Most recent stocking: October 07, 2025.

Since June 2020: 95 total stockings · 228,056 fish · Supplied by Lisboa Springs Trout Hatchery and Red River Trout Hatchery

Special Regulations

Info: From east end of Tolby Campground downstream 1.4 miles to first US 64 bridge

Bag Limit: Catch-and-release only, artificial fly or lure with single barbless hook

Species: rainbow trout, brown trout

Note: This information is sourced from NM Game & Fish GIS data. Always check the official NM Game & Fish fishing regulations for the most current rules.

Heading out? Freshly stocked trout hit PowerBait hard in the first few days. Inline spinners like Panther Martins are another reliable choice for stocked trout. (affiliate links)
